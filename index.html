Author JAne

The new gen-Z author who prefers to stay anonymous and has dipped her feet in different genres like romance,fantasy,mythological,highschool, and what not.She is a easy going cheerful person and is a huge fan of pop music.

She is a coffee and animal lover and is some who prefers to have a novel in one hand and coffee along with good music than any big party in town.

She has written a lot of novels in the five years she has been active in the writing circle though she tends to say that it is more likely because of her friends who always support her to write and help her during her blocks.she travels a lot and gets inspired by the places she visits and the culture and includes them in her books

Her works include 'Say you don't series','Idle Laws of Love','Blessed for Eternity','Cursed for Eternity','A Marriage of convinience','A Summer full of you','Royal archives','B-16273','I didn't know at first',and a few upcoming novels in this year after which she has decided to take a one year break

She instantly rose to fame after her first novel 'Blessed for eternity' it is a fantasy story and the plot runs in a world called as Timinar the story is narrated in the point of views of different people they are the female lead Ruella Evastilion,the male lead Aurelius VanGoth,Vinat Demicritus,and third person ,the book reached a major audience and was a grat sucess ,it had a intricately aligned storyline where each page hooked the readers and the romance got evryone grinning like a cheshire cats

while leaving her readers on hold she released her second novel A Summer full of you a story about a ballet dancer and a F1 race manager a amazing combination of speed and elegance which left the readers captivated to her every word and made a huge hit the story was a great as she shifted genres to fantasy to modern romance
