    • Her personal life

    The author shared that the funny story behind her name JAne Bellarus as a child she loved the name jane and decided for it to be her pen name and when she is in her high school during the final days her friend whom she treated as family was joking about a surname for their family tree decided on bellarus and as for the JA in her name it is because that's the way she gives her sign and felt that there are too many jane's but not JAne so decided it

    Author shared that she has been a big fan of books since chidhood and was always creating stories in her head sometimes short ones and sometimes really long she used think about plots everyday before going to sleep as a hobby.Her favourite novel growing up was Harry Potter Prisoner of Azkaban saying it always manages to make her feel like a kid and manages ingnites her inner child spirit she started reading Comics,Manhwa,Manga,Romance, at the age of 13 and Adult Romance at the age of 15
